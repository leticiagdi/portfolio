5 Stages in the Design Thinking Process

  • Empathize: Review the current SAP CRM design to understand its existing structure and user interaction patterns.
  • Define: Identify areas of improvement by analyzing the old design, such as outdated visual elements and inconsistent user flows.
  • Ideate: Explore how SAP Fiori design principles can enhance the user experience by creating a cleaner, more intuitive interface.
  • Prototype: Refine the existing design using SAP Fiori guidelines and build interactive mockups based on the SAPUI5 framework.
  • Test: Conduct internal reviews and usability tests to ensure the redesigned CRM aligns with SAP's design standards and improves user satisfaction.
