5 Stages in the Design Thinking Process

  • Empathize: Gather feedback from hospital staff to understand their needs and pain points with the current website.
  • Define: Identify key issues such as navigation challenges and outdated content that hinder user engagement and accessibility.
  • Ideate: Brainstorm design solutions that address identified problems, focusing on modern aesthetics and improved functionality.
  • Prototype: Create wireframes and interactive mockups of the new website, showcasing proposed changes in layout and content structure.
  • Test: Gather user feedback on the prototypes, refining designs based on usability tests to ensure an optimal user experience.
